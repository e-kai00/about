about

Hi, I'm Ekaterina, a software developer. I'm a career changer. My initial academic background lies in languages and literature, and later I studied economics. For the past 6 years, I've been immersed in the world of e-commerce. But I always had an interest in technology.

In 2023 I've decided to reshape my career path, enrolling in a Full Stack Software Development program with Code Institute. Through an intensive six-month, full-time course I acquired formal training in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python and laid solid foundations, upon which I continue building my knowledge and skills.

Passionate about both backend and frontend development, I actively pursue continuous learning through various courses. I find joy in participating in hackathons, where I can leverage my skills in collaborative problem-solving. Outside of coding, I like to delve into cybersecurity topics, enriching my understanding of the digital landscape to contribute to robust solutions.
I'm excited to embark on this new chapter in my career and anticipate expanding my expertise in the dynamic realm of software development.
